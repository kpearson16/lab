Falafel

Here's how to make the best Falafel Wrap

Serves: 4 Prep Time: 15 minutes Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 8oz chickpeas
  • onion
  • tomato
  • hummus
  • pita bread
  • tomato
  • garlic powder
  • parsley
  • coriander
  • cumin
  • oil
  • cucumber
  • tahini
  • chili sauce

Directions

  1. Chop onion. Add seasoning powders, fresh herbs and chickpeas to food processor.
  2. Shape the mixture into a ball. Let sit in fridge for 15 minutes.
  3. Add an inch of oil to frying pan on low - medium heat.
  4. Add falafel balls to pan and let fry for 5-7 minutes untl brown on each side.
  5. Remove falafel from pan and blot with kitchen paper to remove excess oil.
  6. For your wrap, take your warmed flatbread and spread hummus on it.
  7. Add falafel and garnish with leafy greens, cucumber, tomatos.
  8. Drizzle tahini sauce.
  9. Enjoy!
